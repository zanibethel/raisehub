# RaiseHub Business Model

## Revenue Philosophy

RaiseHub should never charge businesses for the ability to support their community.

Businesses pay only when they want additional tools that help them grow faster, gain more exposure, or save time.

## Community Partner

The default business account is free.

### Included

- Business profile
- Business logo
- Contact information
- Address and map information
- Website link
- Up to three active offers
- Participation in local fundraising
- Support for all eligible organizations
- Customer-selected organization support
- Basic offer views
- Basic redemption tracking
- Basic business analytics
- Universal QR or code redemption
- Manual redemption when native POS integration is unavailable
- Community Partner badge

The free plan must remain genuinely useful.

## Growth Partner

A paid Growth Partner plan may include:

- More than three active offers
- Priority placement in business listings
- Featured placement on selected pages
- AI marketing credits
- Advanced analytics
- Customer and redemption trends
- Seasonal campaign recommendations
- Additional team members
- Additional locations
- Marketing automation
- Priority support

## AI Credits

AI credits may be used for:

- Coupon ideas
- Offer titles and descriptions
- Facebook posts
- Instagram captions
- Social graphics
- Story graphics
- Email campaigns
- Flyer copy
- Printable promotional materials
- Text-message campaigns
- Seasonal promotion ideas

A limited number of introductory credits may be included for free accounts.

## Features That Must Not Be Paywalled

- Supporting organizations
- Being selected by customers as a participating business
- Basic fundraising participation
- Customer cause selection
- Basic redemption capability
- Basic impact reporting
- Core business profile
- Up to three active offers

## Founding Community Partners

Early participating businesses may receive:

- Founding Community Partner badge
- Early access to new features
- Input into the product roadmap
- Priority onboarding assistance
- Temporary featured placement
- Recognition during the local launch

A founding badge does not necessarily mean all premium features remain free forever.
