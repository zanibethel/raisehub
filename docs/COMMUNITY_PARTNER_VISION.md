# RaiseHub Community Partner Vision

## Purpose

RaiseHub exists to help Community Partners grow their business while giving back to local organizations.

Every successful offer should create three wins:

- The customer saves money.
- The Community Partner gains business.
- The organization raises funds.

---

# Who is a Community Partner?

RaiseHub is intentionally broader than a traditional business directory.

Community Partners include:

- Restaurants
- Food Trucks
- Coffee Shops
- Salons
- Barbers
- Retail Stores
- Gyms
- Automotive Businesses
- Mobile Detailers
- Electricians
- Plumbers
- Lawn Care
- Photographers
- Realtors
- Event Vendors
- Pop-Up Shops
- Farmers Market Vendors
- Seasonal Businesses
- Entertainment Venues
- Professional Services
- Local Attractions

The platform should never assume every partner has a permanent storefront.

---

# Business Types

## Permanent Location

Traditional storefront with consistent hours.

Examples:

- Restaurants
- Salons
- Retail
- Gyms

---

## Mobile Business

Businesses that travel to customers.

Examples:

- Food Trucks
- Mobile Detailing
- Pet Grooming
- Mobile Mechanics

Future capabilities:

- Live location
- Today's schedule
- GPS map integration
- Temporary offers

---

## Event Vendor

Businesses operating at fairs, festivals and sporting events.

Future capabilities:

- Event-specific offers
- Event scheduling
- Event analytics

---

# Business Goals

Every offer should begin with a goal.

Examples:

- Bring in new customers
- Reward loyal customers
- Increase slow-day traffic
- Promote a new product
- Increase average purchase
- Introduce seasonal items
- Fill appointment openings
- Increase event attendance

The AI Assistant should generate offers based on the selected goal.

---

# Food Truck Vision

Food trucks deserve a unique experience.

Instead of asking for only an address, RaiseHub should support:

Today's Location

Operating Hours

Current Event

Weekly Schedule

Customers should eventually be able to discover nearby trucks currently serving.

Future features:

- Live map
- "Open Now"
- Push notifications
- Daily specials
- Event schedule
- Route history

---

# POS Integration

RaiseHub should work with existing checkout systems.

Priority integrations:

- Square
- Clover
- Toast
- Shopify POS
- Stripe Terminal

Businesses should never be forced to replace existing workflows.

RaiseHub should extend existing systems rather than replace them.

---

# Offer Creation Philosophy

Businesses should never start with a blank screen.

Offer creation should guide them through:

Business Goal

Suggested Offers

Customization

Rules

Preview

Publish

Eventually AI should generate:

Offer title

Description

Fine print

Facebook post

Instagram caption

Flyer copy

Email promotion

---

# AI Assistant Vision

The AI Assistant should become a marketing employee for every Community Partner.

Examples:

Generate Offers

Generate Social Posts

Generate Flyers

Generate Email Campaigns

Generate Seasonal Promotions

Generate Event Promotions

Suggest Better Offers

Analyze Redemption Performance

Recommend Improvements

Future capabilities:

- Slow-day recommendations
- Holiday campaigns
- Weather-aware promotions
- Local event awareness
- Customer behavior insights
