# RaiseHub Product Vision

## Mission

RaiseHub helps local businesses grow by making everyday purchases strengthen the local community.

## Core Promise

**Every purchase has a purpose.**

Customers receive useful local offers while choosing which participating organization benefits from their purchase.

## Primary Participants

### Businesses

Businesses join RaiseHub to:

- Attract new customers
- Encourage repeat visits
- Build a stronger local reputation
- Create and promote meaningful offers
- Support local organizations without managing a fundraiser
- Track customer activity and community impact

Joining RaiseHub and supporting organizations must remain free.

### Organizations

Organizations use RaiseHub to:

- Raise money without selling physical products
- Avoid collecting cash or distributing coupon books
- Share their story with the community
- Promote participating local businesses
- Track progress toward clear fundraising goals

Organizations should never be required to pay to participate in the core fundraising experience.

### Customers

Customers use RaiseHub to:

- Discover local businesses
- Receive meaningful discounts and offers
- Choose which local cause benefits
- Track the impact created by their purchases
- Support the community through spending they were already planning

## Product Principles

### 1. Community participation is never paywalled

No feature required for a business to support organizations should require a paid subscription.

### 2. Businesses pay for growth, not generosity

Paid features should help businesses gain additional exposure, create more offers, save time, or understand their customers.

### 3. Customers choose the cause

When possible, the customer should choose which eligible organization receives the fundraising benefit from their purchase.

### 4. Fundraising should feel effortless

Organizations should not need to handle products, collect money, distribute materials, or reconcile paper sales.

### 5. Businesses should receive measurable value

RaiseHub should help businesses understand:

- Customers gained
- Offers viewed
- Offers redeemed
- Returning customers
- Revenue influenced
- Organizations supported
- Community dollars generated

### 6. RaiseHub should fit existing business workflows

Businesses should not be forced to replace their current payment or point-of-sale system.

RaiseHub should support:

- Universal QR or code redemption
- Manual redemption when necessary
- Native integrations where supported
- Square
- Clover
- Toast
- Shopify POS
- Stripe Terminal
- Other commonly used systems over time

### 7. Simplicity comes before feature volume

The core experience should be understandable without a lengthy tutorial.
