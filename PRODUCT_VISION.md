# RaiseHub Product Vision

## Mission

RaiseHub helps schools, teams, churches, nonprofits, and community organizations raise money by partnering with local businesses that offer valuable discounts to supporters.

Instead of selling products that are thrown away or forgotten, RaiseHub creates long-term value for everyone involved.

---

## Businesses

Businesses create promotional offers.

Examples:

- $5 off purchase
- Buy One Get One
- Free appetizer
- 20% off services

Businesses gain:
- New customers
- Repeat customers
- Community exposure
- Measurable campaign participation

---

## Organizations

Organizations launch fundraising campaigns.

Supporters purchase campaigns.

Organizations receive a percentage of every sale.

Campaigns should be easy to create and easy to manage.

---

## Customers

Customers purchase fundraising campaigns.

Purchased offers are permanently saved inside their digital pass until expiration.

Customers should never lose access after purchase.

---

## Design Philosophy

RaiseHub should feel:

- Simple
- Fast
- Friendly
- Community focused
- Mobile first
- Trustworthy

Every feature should reduce friction.

---

## Long Term Vision

RaiseHub becomes the easiest fundraising platform for local communities.

Eventually include:

- QR redemption
- Stripe payouts
- Business analytics
- Organization analytics
- AI marketing tools
- Business verification
- Mobile apps
- Push notifications
- Referral rewards
- Community events

---

## Development Philosophy

Always prioritize:

1. Reliability
2. Security
3. Simplicity
4. Reusable code
5. Great user experience

Avoid unnecessary complexity.
