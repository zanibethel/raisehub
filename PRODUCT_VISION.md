# RaiseHub Product Vision

## Mission

RaiseHub helps schools, teams, churches, nonprofits, and community organizations raise money by partnering with local businesses that offer valuable discounts to supporters.

Instead of selling products that are thrown away or forgotten, RaiseHub creates long-term value for everyone involved.

---

## Businesses

Businesses create promotional offers.

Examples:

- $5 off purchase
- Buy One Get One
- Free appetizer
- 20% off services

Businesses gain:
- New customers
- Repeat customers
- Community exposure
- Measurable campaign participation

---

## Organizations

Organizations launch fundraising campaigns.

Supporters purchase campaigns.

Organizations receive a percentage of every sale.

Campaigns should be easy to create and easy to manage.

---

## Customers

Customers purchase fundraising campaigns.

Purchased offers are permanently saved inside their digital pass until expiration.

Customers should never lose access after purchase.

---

## Design Philosophy

RaiseHub should feel:

- Simple
- Fast
- Friendly
- Community focused
- Mobile first
- Trustworthy

Every feature should reduce friction.

---

## Long Term Vision

RaiseHub becomes the easiest fundraising platform for local communities.

Eventually include:

- QR redemption
- Stripe payouts
- Business analytics
- Organization analytics
- AI marketing tools
- Business verification
- Mobile apps
- Push notifications
- Referral rewards
- Community events

---

## Development Philosophy

Always prioritize:

1. Reliability
2. Security
3. Simplicity
4. Reusable code
5. Great user experience

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

# Product Evolution (July 2026)

RaiseHub has evolved beyond a digital coupon fundraiser.

The long-term vision is to become a community growth platform that helps:

- Businesses grow revenue
- Organizations raise more money
- Customers save more money
- Communities become stronger

## Platform Philosophy

RaiseHub should quietly monitor platform health and recommend meaningful actions.

Businesses should manage offers because performance changed—not because a date changed.

The platform should reduce work, not create work.

## Intelligence Strategy

RaiseHub follows a three-layer intelligence model.

### Layer 1

Rule Engine

Deterministic platform logic.

Examples:

- Offer Health
- Dashboard Alerts
- Review Recommendations
- Business Score
- Platform Health

### Layer 2

Analytics

Historical reporting and trends.

Examples:

- Conversion rates
- Redemption rates
- Growth
- Marketplace trends

### Layer 3

AI

Optional creative assistance.

Examples:

- Marketing copy
- Offer rewrites
- Business summaries
- Promotion ideas

AI should never replace deterministic business rules.

## Dashboard Strategy

Every dashboard should answer three questions.

1. What happened?

2. What needs attention?

3. What should I do next?

This philosophy applies to:

- Customer Dashboard
- Business Dashboard
- Organization Dashboard
- Admin Dashboard
- Owner Dashboard

## Long-Term Goal

RaiseHub becomes the operating system for local community fundraising.
