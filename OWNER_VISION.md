# RaiseHub Owner Vision

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Owner Dashboard provides a complete executive view of the RaiseHub platform.

Unlike the Admin Dashboard, this dashboard focuses on strategy, growth, and
overall platform health.

---

# Marketplace Overview

Display:

- Total Businesses
- Total Organizations
- Total Members
- Active Offers
- Active Fundraisers
- Monthly Growth
- Total Redemptions

---

# Revenue

Track:

- Monthly Revenue
- Annual Revenue
- Stripe Revenue
- Subscription Revenue
- Business Upgrades
- Refunds

Include charts for:

- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Yearly

Businesses with no active offers
